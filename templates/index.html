IMA National Sports Meet | DOCTORS OLYMPIAD 2023

November 22 - 26, 2023 | Andhra Pradesh

Check out this enthralling teaser for the IMA National Sports Meet “DOCTORS OLYMPIAD 2023” ! Get ready for a competition like no other, as medical professionals from all over India come together to show off their athletic prowess and sportsmanship.
